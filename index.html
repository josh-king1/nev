About AAU Navigator

The AAU Smart Campus Navigator is a digital navigation system designed to assist students, staff, and visitors of Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma.

It provides real-time GPS tracking, smart walking routes, distance calculation, and accurate faculty locations to make campus movement easy and stress-free.
